Card family: `Card` (base surface), `ServiceCard`, `PortfolioCard`, `TestimonialCard`.

```jsx
<ServiceCard number="01" icon={<Icon/>} title="Programação" description="Sites e sistemas sob medida." />
<PortfolioCard image="cover.jpg" category="Tráfego Pago" title="Loja XYZ" metric="+312% ROAS" />
<TestimonialCard rating={5} quote="A duPolvo virou o jogo." name="Marina C." role="CEO, Studio Lume" />
```

All cards lift on hover (`Card` needs `hover`). Use `dark` on `Card` for the inverse navy surface.
