Expandable FAQ accordion row. Stack several inside a container.

```jsx
<FaqItem defaultOpen question="Quanto custa?" answer="Orçamento sob medida a partir do escopo." />
<FaqItem question="Qual o prazo?" answer="Projetos entregues em 2 a 6 semanas." />
```

Each item manages its own open state; the plus icon rotates to a cross when open.
