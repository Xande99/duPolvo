Small indicators: `Badge` (status/label chip), `Tag` (filter pill), `Eyebrow` (section kicker).

```jsx
<Eyebrow>Nossos serviços</Eyebrow>
<Badge variant="solid" dot>Novo</Badge>
<Tag interactive active>Design</Tag>
```

`Badge` variants: accent · navy · solid · success · warning · outline · on-dark. `Tag` supports `active`/`interactive`/`onDark`. `Eyebrow` adds an uppercase overline with accent line; use `onDark` over navy.
